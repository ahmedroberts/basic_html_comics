  • Green Lantern John Stewart

    Originally my favourite Green Lantern was John Stewart and I knew more about Kyle Rayner than Hal Jordan but since hearing the hype about the Sinestro Corps War, I read Blackest Night and started collecting the Geoff Johns Green Lantern series. This showed me what a brilliant character Hal is. His determination, cockiness and ability to continue moving forward in spite of his past made for a compelling character arc in my eyes.
